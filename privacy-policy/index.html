🔒 Privacy Policy

Office Reader — Document Viewer & PDF Tools

Overview

Last updated: March 14, 2026

TechLead ("we", "our", or "us") built the Office Reader app as a Free app. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

🛡️ Your privacy matters. Office Reader processes all documents locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to your files.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve app performance and stability:

  • Account information (optional): If you create an account (Sign Up), we collect your email, display name, and optional profile photo. This data is stored on Supabase for sign-in, profile sync, and features like leaderboard. Account creation is optional — you can use the app without an account.
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage statistics such as which features are used, screen views, and app interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports: Technical crash data including device model, OS version, and stack traces to fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device identifiers: Anonymous device IDs for analytics and push notification delivery (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your documents, files, or their contents
  • ❌ Personal information — unless you create an account (then we collect email, name, avatar)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or call logs
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Financial information

Permissions

Office Reader requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To scan, open, and manage documents on your device. Files are processed entirely on-device and never uploaded.
  • Camera: For the document scanner feature. Photos are processed locally and saved only to your device storage.
  • Internet: For Google Drive integration, crash reporting, analytics, and checking for app updates.
  • Notifications: To send you update notifications (opt-in on Android 13+).

Google Drive

When you connect your Google Drive account:

  • We use Google's official API to access your Drive files
  • Authentication is handled directly by Google Sign-In
  • We only access files you explicitly choose to open
  • We do not store your Google credentials
  • You can disconnect your account at any time from the Settings screen

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Supabase — Account authentication and profile data (email, display name, avatar) when you create an account
  • Firebase Analytics — Usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Crash reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — Push notifications
  • Google Play Services — Core Google services
  • Google Drive API — Cloud file access

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • We request consent before collecting analytics data using Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP)
  • You can change your consent preferences at any time
  • You can request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us

Data Security

  • All network connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Documents are processed locally — never transmitted to our servers
  • Account data (email, profile) is stored on Supabase; passwords are hashed
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Office Reader does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
